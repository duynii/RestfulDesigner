Entity or Domain Class Creation

New Entity: Right click on the canvas/background and select 'Create new concept'.
Configuring the entity, click on the 'edit' button to bring up the edit menu.

  1. - Name of entity is editable.
  2. - Adding new property by entering the property name and selecting the data type.
  3. - Selecting the 'belongs to' entity, the select entity is on the one side of one-many relationship
Double click on the property row - highlighted on mouse over - to delete the property.
The 'id' property cannot be deleted.
